FAQ

Questions you might
be holding.

Is it really free?
Yes — and it'll stay free while NovaLog is in early access. If a paid tier comes later (think: more features, team sharing), the basics stay free.
Who can see my ideas?
Only you. Every idea is stored against your account and protected by row-level security on the database. Even the developer can't browse your notes.
Can I export my data?
Yes. In the app sidebar, click Export JSON — you get a full backup of your ideas, tags, and statuses in a single file. Import works the same way.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, fully. Account → Danger zone → Delete my account. Your data is wiped permanently within seconds — there's no soft-delete or shadow archive.
Is the code open source?
The frontend is public on GitHub. You can host your own copy, fork it, or just read how it works.
